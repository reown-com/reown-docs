# Payments

Reown offers a comprehensive Payments toolkit, supporting all levels of users, multiple chains and diverse asset types. Instead of a single rigid flow, Reown provides modular solutions for different financial needs, giving you access to hundreds of millions of users via 600+ wallets and multiple exchanges, with support for EVM, Bitcoin, Solana, Ton and Tron networks.

Reown's all-in-one toolkit for creating seamless payment flows, includes :

1. **Onramps**: Let users buy cryptocurrencies with fiat currencies, like dollars or euros directly in-app.

2. **Swaps**: Let users swap coins and tokens seamlessly, directly in-app.

3. **Pay with Self Custodial Wallet**: Let users pay with their self custodial wallets like MetaMask, Trust, Ledger, and more. This flow is bundled together with the **Pay with Exchange** flow.

4. **Pay with Exchange**: Let users pay directly from Centralized Exchanges (CEXs) like Binance.
