Reown offers a comprehensive Payments toolkit, supporting all levels of users, multiple chains and diverse asset types. Instead of a single rigid flow, Reown provides modular solutions for different financial needs, giving you access to hundreds of millions of users via 600+ wallets and multiple exchanges, with support for EVM, Bitcoin, Solana, Ton and Tron networks.

Reown's all-in-one toolkit for creating seamless payment flows includes:

1. **Onramps**: Let users buy cryptocurrencies with fiat currencies, like dollars or euros directly in-app.

2. **Swaps**: Let users swap coins and tokens seamlessly, directly in-app.

3. **Pay with Self Custodial Wallet**: Let users pay with their self custodial wallets like MetaMask, Trust, Ledger, and more.

4. **Deposit with Exchange**: Let users deposit funds in-app via Centralized Exchanges (CEXs) wallets, like Binance.

## Payments FAQs

### What happened to "Pay with Exchange"?

The Pay with exchange feature has been deprecated due to the evolving challenge of compliance for our partner centralized exchanges. In future, it may be added to WalletConnect Pay. [To learn more about WalletConnect Pay, visit the docs here →](https://docs.walletconnect.com/payments/overview)

### What happened to one-click checkout?

One-click checkout has been migrated to WalletConnect Pay. [To set up seamless crypto payments for your business, visit the WalletConnect Pay docs here →](https://docs.walletconnect.com/payments/overview)

## Reown Payments or WalletConnect Pay? Which solution is better for me?

To understand which solution is better for you, browse and answer these questions for your own business.

<details>
<summary>Are you a web3 app builder or are you looking to accept crypto payments in any other type of business?</summary>

The Reown SDK is designed for web3 app builders, incorporating payments solutions into a wider stack also offering authentication and interaction solutions. WalletConnect Pay is a standalone B2B payments infrastructure product targeting merchants, PSPs, and e-commerce/POS systems — it's purely focused on crypto payment processing.

The bottom line:
- If you're building a web3 app, Reown is likely your best solution
- If you want to accept crypto payments in your online or IRL shop, WalletConnect Pay may suit you better.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Which architecture type suits your business better?</summary>

The Reown SDK's payment solutions are client-initiated: your frontend passes payment parameters (recipient, amount, asset) directly into the SDK, which opens a modal UI and handles the flow. WalletConnect Pay is server-authoritative: a payment intent is created server-side, and the wallet resolves the canonical payment details by calling Gateway endpoints. This means the server defines what the wallet does, not the client, making WalletConnect Pay more tamper-resistant.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What level of compliance do you need?</summary>

WCP includes compliance infrastructure that AppKit doesn't: KYC/AML data collection, sanction screening, travel rule data collection, fiat settlement for merchants, and refunds. It's built to handle regulatory requirements that enterprise payment flows require.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What other features do you need?</summary>

Reown offers broader solutions such as smart accounts, app analytics, funding from existing wallets, hooks and interactions infrastructure and more.

WalletConnect Pay is purely for payments, also offering deeper payment solutions such as merchant reconciliation, fiat off-ramp, POS integration, gateway API, payment history, and whitelabel.

</details>
