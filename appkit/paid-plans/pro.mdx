Reown Pro bridges the gap between Starter and Enterprise tiers, offering more advanced features and analytics for growing teams—without a custom plan, available directly via the Reown dashboard.

## How to Upgrade to Reown Pro

If you're interested in upgrading to Reown Pro, you can self-serve directly via the Reown dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to the Reown Dashboard">
    Go to [dashboard.reown.com](https://dashboard.reown.com/).
  </Step>
  <Step title='Click "Upgrade Now"'>
    Click the **"Upgrade Now"** button in the top right-hand corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Reown Pro">
    Select the **Reown Pro** plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check out via Stripe">
    Complete your purchase through Stripe.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  You will immediately get access to Pro features upon successful checkout. All new Pro subscriptions include **30 days free**, and billing only starts after your free trial concludes.
</Info>

## What can I expect from the Pro plan?

### More RPC calls & MAUs

Start building and iterating for more users and usage, with higher limits for RPC calls and the capacity to handle more Monthly Active Users (MAUs).

- **RPC Calls:** Up to 5 Million (vs 2.5 Million on Starter).
- **MAUs:** Up to 7,500 (vs 500 on Starter).

### Uncapped Overage

Eliminate disruptions as your app grows. Unlike the Starter plan, where projects are paused upon reaching limits, Reown Pro features uncapped overage.

- **Extra MAUs:** $0.05 per extra MAU above 7,500.
- **Extra RPC:** $1 per 100K extra RPC calls over 5 Million.

### More projects & Team members

Manage up to **3 projects** from a single team dashboard and add up to **5 team members**, allowing your entire team to collaborate effectively in one environment.

### Feature Toggles

On the Pro plan, you can enable the following features in your dashboard configuration settings:

- [Authentication](/appkit/authentication/siwx/default): Allow connections to 500+ crypto wallets across all supported networks via the WalletConnect Protocol.
- [Social & Email logins](/appkit/authentication/socials): Provide non-crypto logins via Google, Apple, Github, Facebook and more.
- [Multiwallet Linking](/appkit/features/multiwallet-linking): Allow users to connect multiple wallets to your app in a single session.
- [Onramps](/appkit/features/onramp): Let your users buy crypto via a third-party provider directly in app.
- [Swaps](/appkit/features/swaps): Let your users make crypto to crypto exchanges on all supported networks.
- Event Tracking: Capture in-app events to track data performance in your [Analytics dashboard](/cloud/analytics).
- Activity: Allow your users to view their in-app transaction log.
- Headless: Personalize your connection UI to fit your app's brand aesthetics.

For more information on feature toggles, visit the [dashboard configuration docs](/cloud/dashboard-configuration-settings).

### Advanced analytics

Dive deeper into your data to make informed decisions. Gather 7-day analytics on total users, behavior, and app usage, including:

- **App Performance:** Sessions vs wallet connections, Signatures, Active Hours.
- **User Insights:** Total users, Geographical Distribution.
- **Connections:** Networks, Login methods, Wallet providers.
- **Payments:** Swaps, Onramps, Revenue, swap pairs, Onramp currencies, Geographic distribution of swaps and onramps.
- **Technical insights:** Devices, Operating systems.

### Improved Support

Get assistance when you need it. Access effective support with the ability to create support tickets directly via the Reown dashboard and escalate to a human whenever necessary.
