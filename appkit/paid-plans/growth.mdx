The Growth plan offers enterprise-grade features, such as advanced analytics, funding in-app wallet flows, UI customization, and in-dashboard support; but without the complications of negotiating a custom agreement: you can upgrade directly through the Reown dashboard and receive the features immediately.

## How to Upgrade to the Reown Growth plan

If you're interested in upgrading to the Growth plan, you can self-serve directly via the Reown dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to the Reown Dashboard">
    Go to [dashboard.reown.com](https://dashboard.reown.com/).
  </Step>
  <Step title='Click "Upgrade Now"'>
    Click the **"Upgrade Now"** button in the top right-hand corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Growth">
    Select the **Growth** plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check out via Stripe">
    Complete your purchase through Stripe.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  You will immediately get access to Growth features upon successful checkout. All new Growth subscriptions include **30 days free**, and billing only starts after your free trial concludes.
</Info>

## What can I expect from the Growth plan?

### More RPC calls & MAUs

Start building and iterating for more users and usage, with higher limits for RPC calls and the capacity to handle more Monthly Active Users (MAUs).

- **RPC Calls:** Up to 12.5 Million (vs 5 Million on Pro).
- **MAUs:** Up to 15,000 (vs 7,500 on Pro).

### Uncapped Overage

Eliminate disruptions as your app grows. Unlike the Starter plan, where projects are paused upon reaching limits, Reown Growth features uncapped overage.

- **Extra MAUs:** $0.05 per extra MAU above 15,000.
- **Extra RPC:** $1 per 100K extra RPC calls over 12.5 Million.

### Unlock seamless project management

Manage **unlimited projects** from a single team dashboard and add up to **10 team members**, allowing your entire team to collaborate effectively in one environment.

### Feature Toggles

On the Growth plan, you can enable the following features in your dashboard configuration settings:

- [Authentication](/appkit/authentication/siwx/default): Allow connections to 500+ crypto wallets across all supported networks via the WalletConnect Protocol.
- [Social & Email logins](/appkit/authentication/socials): Provide non-crypto logins via Google, Apple, Github, Facebook and more.
- [Multiwallet Linking](/appkit/features/multiwallet-linking): Allow users to connect multiple wallets to your app in a single session.
- [Onramps](/appkit/features/onramp): Let your users buy crypto via a third-party provider directly in app.
- [Swaps](/appkit/features/swaps): Let your users make crypto to crypto exchanges on all supported networks.
- Funding Wallets: Allow your users to fund in-app wallets with either [exchange wallets](/appkit/payments/deposit-with-exchange) or [self-custodial wallets](/appkit/payments/pay-with-self-custodial-wallets) they already own.
- Event Tracking: Capture in-app events to track data performance in your [Analytics dashboard](/cloud/analytics).
- Activity: Allow your users to view their in-app transaction log.
- Headless: Personalize your connection UI to fit your app's brand aesthetics.
- Remove Reown branding: Remove the "UX powered by Reown" tag at the bottom of your modals.

For more information on feature toggles, visit the [dashboard configuration docs](/cloud/dashboard-configuration-settings).

### Advanced analytics

Dive deeper into your data to make informed decisions. Gather full history, 7-day, 30-day and quarterly reports on users, behavior, and app usage, including:

- **App Performance:** Sessions, Sessions vs wallet connections, Signatures, Active Hours.
- **User Insights:** Total users, Geographical Distribution, Net Worth, App Co-usage, Sanctioned users.
- **Connections:** Networks, Login methods, Wallet providers.
- **Payments:** Swaps, Onramps, Revenue, swap pairs, Onramp currencies, Geographic distribution of swaps and onramps.
- **Technical insights:** Devices, Operating systems.

### Improved Support

Get assistance when you need it. Access effective support with the ability to create support tickets directly via the Reown dashboard and escalate to a human whenever necessary.
