The Starter plan is completely free; designed for early-stage startups that want to test out features, provide wallet connections and get started immediately at no cost.

## How to use the Starter plan

All new users are on the Starter plan by default. Simply sign up or login via the [Reown Dashboard](https://dashboard.reown.com/) to start building on Starter.

---

## What can I expect on the Starter plan?

### 1 Project & 1 Team member

The Starter plan includes 1 project and 1 team member.

### Basic Usage Allowance

- **RPC Calls:** Up to 2.5 Million
- **MAUs:** 500

<Info>
  Exceeding your usage on the Starter tier will pause your project, meaning you will no longer be able to make RPC calls or accept extra MAUs. If you need uncapped overage, please upgrade to the Pro plan.
</Info>

### Feature Toggles

On the Starter plan, you can enable the following features in your dashboard configuration settings:

- [Authentication](/appkit/authentication/siwx/default): Allow connections to 500+ crypto wallets across all supported networks via the WalletConnect Protocol.
- [Social & Email logins](/appkit/authentication/socials): Provide non-crypto logins via Google, Apple, Github, Facebook and more.
- [Onramps](/appkit/features/onramp): Let your users buy crypto via a third-party provider directly in app.
- [Swaps](/appkit/features/swaps): Let your users make crypto to crypto exchanges on all supported networks.
- Event Tracking: Capture in-app events to track data performance in your [Analytics dashboard](/cloud/analytics).
- Activity: Allow your users to view their in-app transaction log.

For more information on feature toggles, visit the [dashboard configuration docs](/cloud/dashboard-configuration-settings).

### Basic analytics

The Starter tier includes basic app analytics for the previous 7 days including:

- **App Performance:** Sessions vs wallet connections
- **User Insights:** Total users
- **Payments:** Total Swaps, Total Onramps

### AI-assisted Support

Starter tier users can ask the in-dashboard support bot any questions on the product or their integration, but do not have the ability to escalate to the human customer experience team or open tickets.
