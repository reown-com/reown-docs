title: WalletGuide Submission
---

Wallet submissions are now handled through the **WalletConnect Dashboard**. To submit a new wallet to the [WalletGuide](https://walletguide.walletconnect.network/) and [Cloud Explorer API](./explorer.md), follow the steps below.

## How to Submit

1. Navigate to the [WalletConnect Dashboard](https://dashboard.walletconnect.com/) and sign in. If you don't have an account yet, please create one before proceeding.
2. Once logged in, go to the "**Projects**" section under **Cloud** and click on "**+ Project**".
3. Give a suitable name for your project, select Wallet (WalletKit) and click the "Create" button.
4. Click on "**WalletGuide**" and follow the instructions to fill in your wallet details and submit for review.

For the full step-by-step guide including project details, submission requirements, and QA testing procedures, please refer to the [WalletConnect WalletGuide Submission documentation](https://docs.walletconnect.network/walletguide/explorer-submission).

## What's Next?

Depending on whether or not your submission met all parameters, you will receive an email from the team with the status of your submission.
If your submission was not accepted, you can make the necessary changes and resubmit your project for review. The reason for rejection will be mentioned in the email and in the dashboard.

In case of any questions, feel free to ask on [Github Discussions](https://github.com/orgs/WalletConnect/discussions/categories/explorer-support).
